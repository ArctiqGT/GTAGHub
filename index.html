Welcome to GTAG Hub

What is GTAG Hub?

GTAG Hub is a Gorilla Tag resource server focused on helping creators, modders, and developers. We provide high-quality sound effects, assets, tools, and support for GTAG-related projects.

What We Provide

  • Gorilla Tag Sound Effects (SFX)
  • 3D Assets & Models
  • Unity-ready Resources
  • Creator Support & Help
  • Community-shared Content
